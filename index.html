The Problem

It's 2am. A user's transaction just failed.

They don't know if their funds are gone. They don't know if it's slippage, a missing approval, or a contract bug. Your support team is asleep so they panic in your Discord, or worse, end up in someone else's DM pretending to help.

Meanwhile scam tokens get shared in your community and nobody catches them in time.

Every unanswered failed transaction message is a trust problem waiting to become a churn problem. Or a phishing victim.

[02:14 AM] tx reverted, no response from support

How it Works

From panic to resolved in three steps

No setup headaches. No new app to learn. It lives where your community already is.

1

User pastes a tx hash or address

Right in your Discord or Telegram. No ticket forms, no waiting in a queue. Just a normal message.

2

HexTriage diagnoses it instantly

Cross-references on-chain data, decodes the exact revert reason, and checks for scam signals across 13+ chains.

3

Resolved, or routed to your team

Most issues get answered on the spot. High-value or sensitive cases are flagged and handed to a human, instantly.

Features

Built specifically for DeFi support

Not a generic chatbot wrapper. Purpose-built diagnostics for the exact problems your users actually hit.
