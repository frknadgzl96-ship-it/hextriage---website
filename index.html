The Problem

It's 2am. A user's transaction just failed.

They don't know if their funds are gone. They don't know if it's slippage, a missing approval, or a contract bug. Your support team is asleep — so they panic in your Discord, or worse, in someone else's DM pretending to "help."

Every unanswered failed-tx message is a trust problem waiting to become a churn problem — or a phishing victim.

How it Works

From panic to resolved in three steps

No setup headaches. No new app to learn. It lives where your community already is.

1

User pastes a tx hash or address

Right in your Discord or Telegram — no ticket forms, no waiting in a queue. Just a normal message.

2

HexTriage diagnoses it instantly

Cross-references on-chain data, decodes the exact revert reason, and checks for scam signals — across 13+ chains.

3

Resolved, or routed to your team

Most issues get answered on the spot. High-value or sensitive cases are flagged and handed to a human, instantly.
